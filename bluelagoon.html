Aquatic Paradises

Explore the stunning waters and caves of Malta.

The Blue Lagoon & Comino Island

📍 Comino Island, Malta

A paradise pool with white sand and cyan waters, once a pirate hideout. The Blue Lagoon is a paradise pool nestled between Comino and the islet of Cominotto. Today, its white sandy seafloor reflects the sunlight to create a glowing cyan surface, making it one of the top locations in Europe for swimming and snorkeling.

The Majestic Blue Grotto

📍 Zurrieq, Malta

System of sea caves with a 30-meter arch, famous for its bright blue glow. Located on the southern cliffs of Malta, the Blue Grotto is a complex system of seven interconnected sea caves. The location is globally famous for its morning phenomenon where light reflects a phosphorescent blue glow onto the cave walls.
