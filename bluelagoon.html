The Blue Lagoon

📍 Comino Island, Malta

The Blue Lagoon is a paradise pool featuring a white sandy seafloor and exceptionally clear, glowing cyan waters. Nestled between the peaceful island of Comino and the islet of Cominotto, it offers some of the most spectacular tropical views in the heart of the Mediterranean Sea.
