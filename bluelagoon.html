The Blue Lagoon

Explore turquoise paradise waters, golden cliffs, and untouched scenic beauty

Crystal Clear Paradise

📍 Comino Island

The Blue Lagoon is a slice of heaven nestled between Malta and Gozo on the small island of Comino. Renowned worldwide for its glowing turquoise shallow water and pure white sand, it stands as the ultimate premium destination for swimming, boat cruising, and capturing unforgettable scenery views.

Turquoise Sanctuary

📍 Comino Cliffs

Surrounded by dramatic limestone cliffs, the lagoon forms a natural, peaceful swimming pool protected from the open Mediterranean sea. Visitors can explore local caves, hike across the scenic nature trails of Comino, or simply relax under the bright Maltese sun.

Island Adventures & Boat Cruises

📍 Comino Bay

Daily boat cruises transport travelers from Malta mainland to this paradise shore. It provides an exceptional experience for snorkeling enthusiasts eager to discover underwater marine life hidden within the clear turquoise bays of the Maltese archipelago.
