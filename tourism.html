Valletta: The Fortress Capital

📍 Valletta

Founded in 1566, Valletta is a UNESCO World Heritage site filled with rich Baroque architecture. This historic fortress capital features spectacular limestone bastions, scenic harbor overlooks, and majestic cathedrals.

Golden Bay Sunset

📍 Mellieha, Northwest Coast

Witness the breathtaking, magical sunset over the Mediterranean horizon. The amber sands reflect the deep orange hues, creating an unforgettable coastal moment and a perfect postcard scene.

St. Julian's Promenade

📍 St. Julian's Bay

A lively coastal pathway bustling with energetic waterfront cafes, modern venues, and local fishing boats. It is the ideal place for peaceful evening walks alongside panoramic views of Spinola Bay.
